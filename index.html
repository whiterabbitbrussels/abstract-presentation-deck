How we reach 1 December.

A disciplined, two track method that safeguards the launch date and keeps both workstreams aligned.

IDENTITY
BRUSSELSWHITERABBIT.EU
04

Positioning and identity.

VISUAL IDENTITY
  • Logo and icon system, all the variants you need across digital and print: favicon, social avatars and banners, plus a simple logo animation.
  • Identity toolkit, brand guidelines covering colour, typography, iconography and imagery.
  • Image style and art direction, a moodboard and imagery direction tied to your messaging.
  • Template suite: PowerPoint master, Word letterhead, report and position paper, minutes and agenda, Canva social, email signature.
  • Brand architecture: how the new identity relates to Friends of Glass and Close the Glass Loop.
VERBAL AND TEXTUAL IDENTITY
  • Tagline and descriptor to carry the new name, for example "The Voice of European Glass Makers".
  • Messaging framework: your value proposition and proof points, tied to your strategic priorities.
  • Tone of voice, with practical guidance and examples.
  • Editorial style guide: spelling convention, glossary, hashtags, and a do and don't word list.
  • Boilerplate copy: short, medium and long descriptions for reuse across channels.
  • Launch roadmap, a one page plan for sequencing the roll out.

WEBSITE
BRUSSELSWHITERABBIT.EU
05

Website restructure and migration.

A simplified feve.org, with a sharper value proposition and the new identity applied. English only, accessible, and live at launch.

STRUCTURE AND EXPERIENCE
  • Information architecture and wireframes on a simpler, clearer structure.
  • UX and key user paths aligned to your stakeholders and priorities.
  • Design applying the new visual identity.
  • Content advisory: what stays, what goes, structure, length and format. You own and supply all content.
TECHNICAL AND MIGRATION
  • CMS implementation, WordPress or comparable, easy for your team to edit.
  • Content migration from the current site.
  • New domain set up, with 301 redirects and SEO carry over from feve.org.
  • Accessibility to WCAG 2.1 AA, plus basic SEO.
  • Launch support: the site goes live with the brand. Optional hosting and maintenance from 2027, quoted separately.
